# Optimizing Your Night Shift: When to Drink Coffee for Maximum Alertness - CoffeePlusThree

*Source*: https://coffeeplusthree.com/when-to-drink-coffee-during-night-shift/
*Fetched*: 2026-03-10T01:56:39Z

The best time to drink coffee when working a night shift is about 30 minutes to 1 hour before the shift starts or during the first break. This allows the caffeine to take effect and provide maximum alertness during the most critical periods of the shift.
