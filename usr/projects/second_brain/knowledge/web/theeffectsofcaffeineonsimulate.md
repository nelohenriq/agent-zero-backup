# The effects of caffeine on simulated night-shift work and subsequent daytime sleep - PubMed

*Source*: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/7761739/
*Fetched*: 2026-03-10T01:56:39Z

Subjects adhered to a simulated night-shift schedule for 5 consecutive nights. On the first 3 nights, 2 mg/kg caffeine was added to decaffeinated coffee at 2220 and 0120 hours for the caffeine group.
