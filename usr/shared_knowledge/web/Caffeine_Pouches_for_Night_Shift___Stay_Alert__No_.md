# Caffeine Pouches for Night Shift | Stay Alert, No Crash

*Source*: https://nectr.energy/blogs/life-hacks/caffeine-pouches-for-night-shift-workers-stay-alert-without-the-crash
*Fetched*: 2026-03-10T02:08:26.996080Z

Caffeine has a ~5 hour half-life, so timing your last dose is critical for daytime sleep Small, frequent doses beat one large dose for sustained alertness on shift Sublingual pouches work faster than coffee and don't require a break to drink Switching to zero-caffeine options in the last 2-3 hours of shift protects your sleep
