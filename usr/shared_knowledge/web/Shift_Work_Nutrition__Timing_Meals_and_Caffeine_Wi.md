Workdays can run on a simple template that repeats anchor meals, a predictable caffeine window, and a light post-shift reset that signals the body to power down. Off days don’t need to be chaotic. Keeping sleep within a reasonable range and using the same first meal after waking helps prevent the Monday-whiplash feeling. Rotating schedules are where small adjustments win. Instead of reinventing everything, shift one piece at a time across the week: move bedtime in small steps, slide caffeine earlier, and then nudge meals to match the new wake period.
