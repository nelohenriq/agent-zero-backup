# Demographics, sleep, and daily patterns of caffeine intake of shift workers in a nationally representative sample of the US adult population - PubMed

*Source*: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31628471/
*Fetched*: 2026-03-10T01:56:39Z

Regardless of work schedule, individuals consumed the most caffeine during morning hours. Evening- and night-shift workers reported consuming 36%-46% less caffeine during their work hours and 72%-169% more during nonwork hours than day-shift workers (p < .01).
