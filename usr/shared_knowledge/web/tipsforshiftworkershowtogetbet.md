# Tips for Shift Workers: How To Get Better Sleep | Sleep Foundation

*Source*: https://www.sleepfoundation.org/shift-work-disorder/tips
*Fetched*: 2026-03-10T01:56:39Z

Moderate amounts of caffeine every one to two hours will be more effective than heavy amounts. You should avoid consuming caffeine within three to four hours of the time you plan to go to sleep.
